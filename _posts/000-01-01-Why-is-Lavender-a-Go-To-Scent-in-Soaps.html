--- layout: post title: "Why is Lavender a Go-To Scent in Soaps?" subtitle: "" date: 000-01-01 00:00:00 -0400 background: '/img/posts/gradient.jpg' --- Lavender is a popular scent in soaps because it has a calming and relaxing aroma that many people enjoy. It is known for its soothing properties, which can help reduce stress and promote relaxation. Additionally, lavender has natural antibacterial qualities, making it beneficial for cleansing the skin. Its pleasant fragrance and skin-friendly properties make lavender a favorite choice for soap makers.
